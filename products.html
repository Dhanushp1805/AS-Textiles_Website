Lightweight Knits

  • Breathable & lightweight

  • Optional Lycra stretch

Common Applications

  • Base layers

Rib & Trim Knits

Rib knits are designed to provide elasticity, recovery, and structure, making them essential for garment finishing and trims.

Terry & Fleece Knits

Terry and fleece knits deliver warmth and softness through looped or brushed constructions, making them ideal for colder climates and relaxed apparel.

Textured / Waffle Knits

Waffle knits feature textured surfaces that improve airflow and moisture management while adding visual depth.

Structured Double Knits

Structured double knits provide stability, thickness, and a smooth finish on both sides, offering a refined look and feel.
