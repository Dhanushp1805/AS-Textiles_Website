Who We Are

AS Textiles is a specialized fabric manufacturer creating premium knitted fabrics for fashion, activewear, and lifestyle apparel. Guided by a commitment to quality, consistency, and innovation, we partner with both emerging designers and established global brands.

Our collection spans lightweight jerseys, breathable performance knits, refined ribs, soft terry and fleece, textured waffles, and structured double knits—each developed with careful attention to hand feel, durability, and finish.

At AS Textiles, fabric is the foundation of design. Through advanced knitting technology and meticulous process control, we deliver fabrics that elevate garments and support modern apparel creation with confidence and precision.
