About AS Textiles

Since 2005, AS Textiles has been operating as a sole proprietorship firm with its head office on Kongu Main Road, Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in the manufacturing of recycled knitted fabrics, catering to the evolving needs of the textile and garment industry.

With the support of our technically advanced production facility and skilled workforce, we produce a wide range of high-quality textile products. Our strong infrastructure and experienced team enable us to meet customer requirements efficiently while maintaining strict quality standards.

We continuously adapt to changing industry trends and focus on sustainable manufacturing practices, delivering dependable and eco-friendly textile solutions to our clients.
